The Cambridge Lovelace Hackathon 2015

December 2015 sees the 200th anniversary of the birth of Ada Lovelace, the mathematician and writer. She saw in computers, and particularly the amazing Analytical Engine, possibilities far beyond the arithmetic manipulation of numbers. In order to celebrate this vision and her remarkable life, we're holding a hackathon in Cambridge, UK in December.

Details are not yet confirmed, but will appear here shortly after they are known. Until then, we encourage you to get involved by joining cam_lovelace, our public mailing list.
