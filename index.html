The Cambridge Ada Lovelace Hackathon 2015

December 2015 sees the 200th anniversary of the birth of Ada Lovelace, the mathematician and writer, and self-identified ‘analyst (& metaphysician)’. She saw in computers, and particularly the amazing Analytical Engine, possibilities far beyond the arithmetic manipulation of numbers. In order to celebrate this vision and her remarkable life, we're holding a hackathon in Cambridge, UK in December.

Details are not yet confirmed, but will appear here shortly after they are known.
